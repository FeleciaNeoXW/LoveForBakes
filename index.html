CAKES

A sweet food made from a mixture of flour, fat, egg, sugar and other ingredients, baked in an oven and sometimes iced or decorated. It may be large and cut into slices or small and intended for one person only.

COOKIES

A sweet biscuit made from stiff, sweet dough rolled and sliced or dropped by spoonfuls on a large, flat pan (cookie sheet) and baked.

BREAD

A kind of food made of flour or meal that has been mixed with milk or water, made into a dough or batter, with or without yeast or other leavening agent, and baked.
